Maybe you’ve been feeling the urge to do something – but you aren’t sure what. Maybe you feel overwhelmed. Maybe you wish that you could have a say in the policies and do something that impacts millions of people around the globe.   Well, now you can. People all over the world engage in volunteerism for a great variety of reasons. Somehow, in the end, volunteerism makes a specific contribution by generating well-being for people and their communities.

Become a volunteer

Raise your voice and show your energy

With every hour of their time, Grow Together volunteers multiply their impact through the enormous power of advocacy — whether it’s helping change policy to support millions of families putting food on the table or helping raise billions of dollars for the world’s most vulnerable children.Volunteers are well-placed to support poverty reduction strategies and create pathways to the economic empowerment of women and youth.

As a volunteer you will:

  • Develop relationships with your members of Congress so that when you get in touch, they listen
  • Meet face-to-face with your members of Congress and their staff
  • Write persuasive opinion pieces and get published in the media
  • Be part of a movement of people committed to ending the systemic and structural injustices that drive poverty

No previous experiences? No worries.

Grow Together provides training and coaching to all of our advocates.

Our volunteer advocates come from all walks of life but share a desire to stand up for issues they care about, be heard, and make an impact. At Grow Together, we teach you the nuts and bolts of how to do that. We teach you how to become an advocate. Volunteer with Grow Together and make a real difference in ending poverty!
